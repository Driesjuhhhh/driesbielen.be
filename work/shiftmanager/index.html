About this project

ShiftManager is a practical tool for managing schedules and worker points in organizations such as youth houses and sports clubs. It simplifies planning shifts while tracking contributions through a points system.

Key features

  • Shift creation and scheduling
  • Worker management and assignment
  • Point tracking system
  • Time logging
  • Organization dashboard

Challenges

Designing an interface that makes complex shift scheduling feel straightforward for non-technical users.

What I learned

I learned more about turning operational workflows into tools that are genuinely useful in day-to-day organization management.

ShiftManager is part of the portfolio of Dries Bielen, a full stack developer from Belgium.
