About this project

Streetmasters is a professional website I built for a car cleaning business. It presents services, pricing details, and contact options in a clear way so potential customers can quickly understand the offer and get in touch.

Key features

  • Service showcase
  • Pricing display
  • Contact information
  • Service booking inquiry
  • Responsive design

Challenges

Creating an attractive and professional website for a local business with a tight budget.

What I learned

I learned more about business website design, conversion-focused layouts, and user-focused web development.
